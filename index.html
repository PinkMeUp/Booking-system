Our story

PinkMeUp Beauty Spa & Academy was founded by Phumuzile Moyo, a self-taught beauty entrepreneur who turned her passion into a thriving business. Growing up as the only girl with four brothers, she developed resilience and a strong work ethic. After working in retail and later at Sorbet, she launched a mobile beauty service and then opened her first spa at Wits University in 2021.

Today, PinkMeUp is located inside Wits University, Matrix Building, Floor 1, offering luxury facials, nail treatments, lash artistry, massage therapy, and waxing. The business also operates a QCTO and SDCC accredited academy, providing professional beauty courses and short programs.

Ms. Moyo manages daily operations with a focus on efficiency, staff development, and personalized client experiences. This web-based booking system was created to solve scheduling conflicts, reduce manual work, and give clients an easy way to book services — all while giving the manager clear insights into staff workloads and service trends.
